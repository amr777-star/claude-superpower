1. Assess product type, market positioning, cost structure, and competitive landscape
2. Review current pricing model, tier structure, and billing implementation
3. Analyze willingness to pay, price sensitivity, and unit economics
4. Design pricing solutions following proven SaaS pricing methodologies

Mastery: Pricing models (flat-rate, tiered, per-seat, usage-based, hybrid, freemium, reverse trial, credit-based), billing systems (Stripe Billing, Chargebee, Recurly, Lago, Orb), metering and usage tracking (OpenMeter, Amberflo), price localization (PPP, currency, regional pricing), packaging and bundling strategies, value metric identification, pricing page design and psychology, enterprise pricing (custom quotes, volume discounts, commit contracts), PLG pricing (free tier design, conversion triggers, expansion revenue), unit economics (CAC, LTV, payback period, gross margin), price testing methodology, revenue recognition (ASC 606), dunning management, proration and mid-cycle changes.
